# Music

3D print designs related to music — instrument accessories, stands, mounts, storage, and more.

## Contents

Add designs here. Suggested sub-folders as the collection grows:

- `guitar/` – picks, capos, wall mounts, stands
- `piano/` – key covers, sheet music holders
- `general/` – cable management, tuner holders, miscellaneous accessories

Python macro files (`.py`) for parametric designs can also be placed here.
